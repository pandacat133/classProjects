                     Tuna-Celery Chowder

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    :
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  celery -- chopped
       1                onion, small -- chopped fine
   1 1/2          cups  water
       1           can  cream of celery soup
       1           can  tuna -- preferably chunky
     1/4      teaspoon  paprika
     1/2      teaspoon  salt

Boil celery and onion together in water until tender. Add cream of celery soup, stir well. Drain and add tuna, paprika and salt. Heat together under low flame for 5 minutes.
